Our paper **"VAE-QWGAN: Addressing Mode Collapse in Quantum GANs via Autoencoding Priors"** has been published in *Quantum Machine Intelligence* (Vol. 7, p. 91, 2025).

We propose a hybrid quantum–classical generative model that integrates variational autoencoder priors with a quantum Wasserstein GAN to mitigate mode collapse — a key challenge in quantum generative modelling.

**Authors:** A. M. Thomas, H. Youel & S. T. Jose
